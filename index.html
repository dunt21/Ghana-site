Top three places to visit in Ghana

Elmina castle

This is one of the castles built by the bristish in the Central region when they came to settle at the coastal region in Ghana

Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum

This is a beautiful park built in the Greater Accra region to commerate the first president of Ghana, namely Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

Nzulezu village

This is a town that has been firmly built on a water and has been in good shape for many years, located in the western region
